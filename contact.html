How can I contact support?

If you have a question, the best way to get help is via the chat bubble at the bottom-right of your screen.

Contacting support

You can contact support by clicking the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of the Help Center.

You'll first be connected to an AI assistant that can help resolve common issues. If needed, you can request to speak with a human agent.

Hi! How can I help you today?
I need help with my account
Sure — can you tell me more about the issue?
What to include in your request
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Date and time when the issue occurred
  • Your account email (never share passwords)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Device, browser, and OS details
Troubleshooting tips
  • Refresh the page
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try incognito mode
  • Disable browser extensions
